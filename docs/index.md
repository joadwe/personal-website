## About Me

I'm a Senior Staff Scientist in the Advanced Technology Group at **Waters Biosciences**, developing next-generation instrumentation, software, and assays for reproducible science and medicine. My expertise spans flow cytometry, standardization, data visualization, extracellular vesicles, and scientific education.

Previously, I was at **BD Biosciences** and the **National Institutes of Health (NIH/NCI)**. I received my PhD in Translational Medicine from the **University of Southampton** and my BSc from the **University of Leicester**.
